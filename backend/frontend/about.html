Our Story

When I started homeschooling my own kids, I quickly discovered that one of the most overwhelming parts wasn't the teaching itself — it was choosing a curriculum. There are hundreds of options out there, each with different teaching styles, worldviews, grade ranges, and price points. I spent weeks researching, reading forums, watching YouTube reviews, and still felt unsure.

I knew I wasn't alone. Homeschooling is growing rapidly, and every year thousands of new families face the same overwhelming decision. So I built MyHomeschoolCurriculum — a place where families can compare curricula side by side, filter by what matters most to them, and read honest reviews from real parents.

Our Mission

To ease the stress on new and experienced homeschool families by providing a trusted hub for homeschool curriculum information, community, and resources. We believe choosing a curriculum shouldn't feel like a full-time job — it should feel empowering.

How the Site Works

We research and list curricula across all grade levels, teaching styles, and worldviews. Parents can filter, compare side-by-side, take our recommendation quiz, and read reviews from other families. Our goal is to give you the information you need to make a confident decision.

Our Commitment to Transparency

  • Parent reviews are always independent — publishers cannot edit or remove them
  • Some listings include affiliate links, which means we may earn a small commission if you purchase — at no extra cost to you
  • Sponsored and featured listings are clearly marked so you always know what you're looking at
  • Our written curriculum descriptions and quiz recommendations are editorially independent
